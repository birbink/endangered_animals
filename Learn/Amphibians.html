Axolotl

Nature Danger Score: Critically Endangered

The Axolotl, sometimes called the "walking fish" is a cute little amphibian that is from Mexico. The red frills on its head are actually its gills, which are extrenal. Axolotls have the crazy ability to regenerate not just limbs, but their brain! They can regenrate their limbs more than one-hundred times! Sadly, the Axolotl population was nearly wiped out by the building of cities in Mexico. Luckily, this population is still surviving!
